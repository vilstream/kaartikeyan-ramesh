DESIGN AND FABRICATION OF HUB AND UPRIGHT

2D CAD Drawing

The project is focused on efficiently transferring power from the drive output to the wheel, while significantly reducing weight and improving adjustability.

Objectives

  • Efficient power transfer from output drive to wheel.
  • Reduced component weight with enhanced adjustability.
  • Cost-effective manufacturing and procurement.

Scope & Benefits

  • Rapid and precise manufacturing methods.
  • Strategic weight reduction through optimized bearing size and slotting.
  • Enhanced durability with built-in bearing lock mechanism.
