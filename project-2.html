Design and Manufacturing of Converting the Conventional Lathe Machine to Automated CNC

SolidWorks 3D

This project involves designing and manufacturing a three-axis automated CNC lathe machine capable of precise machining, beneficial to both large-scale and small-scale industries.

Results

  • Successfully automated conventional lathe enhancing accuracy and repeatability.
  • Implemented CNC systems in small industries achieving significant cost savings and improved productivity.
  • Reduced human error by approximately 15%, significantly improving reliability.

Highlights & Benefits

  • Precision machining with high repeatability.
  • Cost-effective transition from manual to automated CNC operations.
  • Smart scalability for small to medium-scale industry growth.
